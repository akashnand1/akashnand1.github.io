03

How I work

Adoption is the product. A feature that ships but is not used is inventory. I design for the second week of use, not the launch demo.

AI earns autonomy per workflow. Nothing an assistant does touches a customer, money or a system of record without a measured baseline, a confidence threshold and a human path for the ambiguous cases.

Explain or be re-checked. If automation cannot give its reason in one line, operations will double-check everything and you have automated nothing.

The team is the roadmap. Ten product managers who can each hold a room with engineering and a CFO will outbuild any individually brilliant backlog.
